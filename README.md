## 🎮 How to Play

1. Choose your **mode** (Time, Words, Quote, Zen).
2. Select **duration** or **word count** / **quote length**.
3. Pick **difficulty** (Easy or Hard).
4. Press **Space** or **Enter** to begin.
5. Type the displayed text as accurately and quickly as you can.
6. Use **Backspace** to correct mistakes.
7. After completion, view your detailed results.
8. Press **Restart** (or ⌘R) to try again.

---

## 🔊 Sound

- Correct keystroke → high-pitched click.
- Incorrect keystroke → low-pitched thud.
- Test finished → ascending chime.
- Click the **Sound** button in the header to toggle audio on/off.

---

## 📱 Browser Support

Works on all modern browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge). For the best experience, use a desktop or laptop with a physical keyboard.
